Browse the process documents below in the order they appear in the project lifecycle, or jump directly to the section most relevant to your current work. Each document provides detailed guidance, checklists, and templates for its respective phase.

## OctoAcme Project Management Process Overview

OctoAcme runs projects with an end-to-end process that covers the full delivery lifecycle:

- **Initiation**: Define the problem statement, identify stakeholders, establish success criteria, and confirm a go/no-go decision before committing resources.
- **Planning**: Build the backlog, identify dependencies, estimate scope, set milestones, and align the team on a release plan.
- **Execution & Tracking**: Use project boards to track progress, follow code quality and testing standards, and escalate blockers through clear paths to resolution.
- **Risks & Communication**: Maintain a risk register, communicate regularly with stakeholders through agreed cadences, and follow escalation procedures when issues arise.
- **Release & Deployment**: Follow release checklists, coordinate deployment steps, prepare rollback plans, and announce releases to stakeholders.
- **Retrospective & Continuous Improvement**: Run structured retrospectives, log action items, and update processes based on learnings to continuously improve delivery.
- **Roles & Personas**: Define and clarify responsibilities for the Project Manager (PM), Product Manager (PdM), Developers, QA, and Stakeholders to ensure clear ownership.
